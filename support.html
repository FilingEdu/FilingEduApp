Frequently Asked Questions

Is FilingEdu free? Yes. All core features — filing search, AI analysis, Learn Center, and Quiz — are free. The free tier includes ads and 3 AI analyses per day.

What is the daily AI analysis limit? Free users get 3 AI filing analyses per day. The limit resets at midnight.

Where does the filing data come from? All SEC filing data is sourced directly from the SEC's EDGAR database (edgar.sec.gov), which is publicly available and updated in real time.

Is this financial advice? No. FilingEdu is an educational platform. We teach you how to interpret SEC filings — we do not provide financial advice, trading signals, or investment recommendations. Always conduct your own due diligence before making any investment decision.

How do I delete my data? FilingEdu does not collect personally identifiable information. If you'd like to request deletion of any anonymous data associated with your device, email us at support@filingedu.com.

The app isn't loading / something looks broken Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, email support@filingedu.com with a description of what you're seeing and your device model.
